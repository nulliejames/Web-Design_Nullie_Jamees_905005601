The Sierra Leone Football Association Premier League

Welcome to the official home of Sierra Leone football! The Sierra Leone Football Association (SLFA) is the governing body of football in Sierra Leone, responsible for organizing and developing the beautiful game across the nation. Our Premier League represents the pinnacle of Sierra Leonean football, featuring the country's most talented players and most passionate clubs. From Freetown to Bo, Makeni to Kenema, we bring communities together through the universal language of football.

WELCOME TO SLFA

Sierra Leone Football Association (SLFA)

Founded in 1960, SLFA has been at the forefront of developing football talent in Sierra Leone. We oversee national teams, league competitions, youth development programs, and grassroots initiatives that nurture the next generation of Leone Stars. Our mission is to promote football as a tool for national unity, youth empowerment, and international recognition.
